# 🗓 5-Day Ramadan Plan (Efficient & Optimal)

## Structure

* Day 1: Push (Chest/Shoulders/Triceps)
* Day 2: Pull (Back/Biceps)
* Day 3: Legs (Strength Focus)
* Day 4: Upper Hypertrophy
* Day 5: Lower + Core + Conditioning

---

# 🔥 DAY 1 – PUSH STRENGTH

**1️⃣ Barbell Bench Press**
4 × 5 reps
Rest 2–3 min
Heavy but clean reps

**2️⃣ Incline Dumbbell Press**
3 × 8

**3️⃣ Seated Dumbbell Shoulder Press**
3 × 6–8

**4️⃣ Cable Lateral Raise**
3 × 12

**5️⃣ Triceps Pushdown**
3 × 10–12

Time target: ~70 min

---

# 🔵 DAY 2 – PULL STRENGTH

**1️⃣ Lat Pulldown or Pull-ups**
4 × 6–8

**2️⃣ Barbell or Machine Row**
4 × 6–8

**3️⃣ Face Pull**
3 × 12

**4️⃣ Dumbbell Hammer Curl**
3 × 10

**5️⃣ Cable Curl**
3 × 10–12

Optional:
5–8 min incline treadmill walk

---

# 🦵 DAY 3 – LEGS (Heavy Base)

**1️⃣ Barbell Squat**
4 × 5

If tired → Leg Press 4 × 8

**2️⃣ Romanian Deadlift**
3 × 6–8

**3️⃣ Walking Lunges**
3 × 8 each leg

**4️⃣ Leg Curl Machine**
3 × 10

**5️⃣ Calf Raises**
3 × 12–15

No cardio today.

---

# 🟣 DAY 4 – UPPER HYPERTROPHY (Moderate Volume)

Less heavy, more controlled.

**1️⃣ Machine Chest Press**
3 × 10

**2️⃣ Cable Row**
3 × 10

**3️⃣ Dumbbell Lateral Raise**
3 × 15

**4️⃣ Rear Delt Fly**
3 × 15

**5️⃣ Superset:**

* Triceps Rope Pushdown ×12
* Dumbbell Curl ×12
  (3 rounds)

Short rest 60 sec.

---

# 🟢 DAY 5 – LOWER + CORE + LIGHT CONDITIONING

**1️⃣ Leg Press**
3 × 10

**2️⃣ Hip Thrust**
3 × 8

**3️⃣ Leg Extension**
3 × 12

**4️⃣ Plank**
3 × 45 sec

**5️⃣ Hanging Leg Raise or Cable Crunch**
3 × 12

Finish:
10 minutes incline treadmill (moderate pace)
