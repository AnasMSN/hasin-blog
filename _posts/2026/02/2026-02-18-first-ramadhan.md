# 🕘 When to Train (Very Important)

Since you train **after breaking fast at night**, ideal timing:

**Option A (Best for performance):**

* Break fast
* Light meal (dates + water + protein + small carbs)
* Pray
* Gym 60–90 min after

**Option B (If tired):**

* Light gym session
* Big meal after gym

For strength focus → Option A is better.

High protein. Moderate carbs. Moderate fat.
